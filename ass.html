Magical Munnar

Munnar, in Kerala’s Western Ghats, is known for its rolling tea gardens, misty mountains, and tranquil scenery.

Top Highlights

  • Tea Plantations across lush hills
  • Eravikulam National Park and rare Nilgiri Tahr
  • Chilly waterfalls like Attukal & Lakkam
  • Sunrise from Top Station viewpoint

Glorious Goa

Goa offers a mix of tropical beaches, Portuguese history, vibrant parties, and seafood delicacies.

My Favorites

  • Watching sunsets at Anjuna Beach
  • Shopping at flea markets
  • Visiting Aguada Fort and churches
  • Jet skiing and parasailing!

Mesmerizing Manali

Manali, nestled in Himachal Pradesh, is a snowy paradise and adventure hub perfect for mountain lovers.

What I Loved

  • Skiing in Solang Valley
  • Cozy cafes with mountain views
  • Paragliding over the Beas River
  • Walking through Old Manali village
